GAME RULES:

** The game has 2 players, playing in rounds - In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes. Each result gets added to his ROUND score.
*** BUT, if the player rolls a 1, all his ROUND score gets 0. After that, it's the next player's turn automatically.
** The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score and he is willingly handing over dice to opponent player.
** at any time by clicking at New Game you can restart the game again if you think luck is not in your favour 😋
*** The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
